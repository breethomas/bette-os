# Weekly Review

Interactive review of the week. Discuss findings with {PM Name}.

## Context Files

Read these first:
- `{Home Directory}/BACKLOG.md` — current items
- `{Home Directory}/GOALS.md` — quarterly priorities

## Workflow

### 1. What got done this week?

Review BACKLOG.md completed items, recent session notes in `{Home Directory}/sessions/`, and any context from the conversation.

### 2. What didn't get done? Why?

Surface items that were P0/P1 at the start of the week but didn't move. Ask {PM Name} why — is it a capacity issue, priority shift, or blocker?

### 3. What's coming next week?

Look at upcoming items in BACKLOG.md, any deadlines, and recurring commitments.

### 4. Goal alignment check

Are current priorities still aligned with GOALS.md? Has anything shifted?

### 5. Prune BACKLOG.md

- Archive completed items
- Remove stale items (ask {PM Name} first)
- Resurface anything that's been sitting too long

### 6. Surface patterns

- What's taking more time than expected?
- What's falling through cracks?
- Any recurring themes?

## Output Style

- Interactive conversation, not a report dump
- Ask questions, don't just present findings
- Be direct about misalignment between stated priorities and actual time spent
